MY PET BARKY

As they say a Dog is man's best friend, Barky is more than a friend and its more like family.
Barky came into my life on my 10th birthday and i can boldy say from that day my was never the same.
Barky is a cold,shy but rough dog when you meet it for the first but lovely,playful and funny when its gets to know you.

My dad got Barky from a pet shop called petco.com when they held an adoption event.
It has a brownish gold cuddly fur back with a white flat tummy and a black nose coupled with white swirl on the face.
Barky's aggressive and rough nature made everyone afraid to adopt it but my dad saw it was behaving that way because it was deprived of love.
